# Loading Completed Jobs

All completed transcription jobs are saved to the local database and remain accessible in the **Transcription History** table on the home screen.

## How to Load a Completed Job

1. On the home screen, locate the job in the **Transcription History** table. Completed jobs show a `complete` status badge.
2. Click `Load` in the job's **Actions** column.
3. The application switches to the **Results** view, showing all transcribed segments for that job.

## Results View

The Results view displays:

- The audio file name as the page heading.
- A segment count subtitle (for example, `42 segment(s)`).
- A table of segments with **Speaker**, **Start**, **End**, and **Content** columns.

From the Results view you can:

- [Edit the transcript](editing_transcripts.md) — review and correct text, adjust timing, merge or split segments, and verify segments while listening to the audio.
- [Edit speaker names](editing_speaker_names.md) — replace generic IDs such as `speaker_0` with real names.
- [Export the transcript](exporting_results.md) — save the transcript to Excel, CSV, JSON, SRT, Markdown, Word, or SQLite.

To return to the history list, click `← Back to History`.
